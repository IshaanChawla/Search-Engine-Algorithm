  <h1 itemprop="name" class="page-title" >Casting Your Content to the Big Screen</h1>
  
  <div itemprop="articleBody">
  

<div>

<div style="float:left; width:44%; margin-right:30px">
<p>Google Cast is a technology that enables multi-screen experiences and lets a user send and control content like video from a small computing device like a phone, tablet, or laptop to a large display device like a television.</p>

<p>The sender may be a phone or tablet running on Android or iOS, or it may be a laptop computer running Chrome. A sender application running on the sender device uses the Google Cast API for the appropriate platform to discover and communicate with the receiver application running on the receiver device. You can use the sender APIs to enable your Android, iOS, or Chrome app to send content to a large display.</p>

<p>The receiver device is optimized for video playback with a receiver application that receives data over Internet Protocol and transmits it to the television. The receiver API lets you customize the messaging between the sender and receiver applications for authentication and other scenarios.</p>
