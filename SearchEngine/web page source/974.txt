<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
According to latest news published in Hindustan Times , UGC has made two important decisions-<br />
1)Candidates can take carbon prints of their optical reader answer sheets with them.<br />
2)Paper-III also will be of objective type.Till now only two papers were objective and paper-3 was subjective.<br />
The new format will be applicable for June 2012 exam and the results will be declared by Oct 2012.<br />
