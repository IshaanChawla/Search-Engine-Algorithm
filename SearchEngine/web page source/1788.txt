But with Labs you'll get immediate help when you need it (no more painstaking hours getting stuck on your own), personalized feedback, and industry context from&nbsp;experienced TAs. In our experience, working with a community of motivated peers will help keep you on track to accomplish your goals, and setting aside time to gather in a room makes&nbsp;committing to learn more rewarding.</p><h3>What does the curriculum look like?</h3><p>Starting with HTML &amp; CSS to build static web pages, you will quickly move on to making your web pages interactive using JavaScript and jQuery. You will dive deep into Ruby on Rails to build robust and production-ready apps, test out Angular.JS, and get introduced to the tools working devs use every day.&nbsp;</p><h3>WHO ARE MY TEACHING ASSISTANTS?</h3><p>We have selected knowledgable and enthusiastic TAs who&nbsp;also learned to code in non-traditional educational environments, often at our partner&nbsp;bootcamps. If you're elsewhere, stay tuned. We might be coming to a city near you&nbsp;in the coming months.&nbsp;</p><h3> </h3><p> </p></div></div></div><div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6"><div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_5_1425941103994_65226"><div class="sqs-block-content"><h3> </h3><h3>WHEN DOES IT START?</h3><p>The start dates depend on where you are based and you should check our schedule. If you don’t see a date that works for you listed, keep an eye out. We’ll be releasing more dates soon.</p><h3>HOW MUCH TIME COMMITMENT IS REQUIRED?</h3><p>Labs classes run 6 hours a week. On top of that, you'll be expected to complete&nbsp;around&nbsp;10 hours of work online on your own outside of class, for a total of 15 weekly hours.&nbsp;</p><h3>WHAT EXPERIENCE DO I NEED?</h3><p>None, nada, nothing! Our class is built to give absolute beginners a comprehensive introduction to web development.&nbsp;</p><h3>How are Labs different from a bootcamp?</h3><p>Codecademy Labs have a greater emphasis on independent online learning. Complete work on your own time and meet for weekly, in-person sessions to stay on track. Labs are a cheaper alternative to immersive learning, but nonetheless provide the value of community support.</p><h3>HOW IS CODECADEMY LABS DIFFERENT FROM CODECADEMY ONLINE?</h3><p>Labs&nbsp;combines&nbsp;Codecademy online with the aspects we most like about the traditional classroom, including group work and expert help.
