<h1 class="subtitle">System Design/Architecture, Java EE, Ajax, jQuery, GWT, &amp; JSF 2, PrimeFaces, Android, Hadoop, etc.</h1>
<p>
  We provide a number of ways to help you in your software development projects:
</p><ul>
  <li><b>Customized onsite training.</b> We give practical, hands-on training right at your location 
    that is customized based on the backgrounds and needs of your developers. Available at any location worldwide. 
    We can also give a crash course on general Java 7 programming as a precursor to more advanced courses on Web apps with JSF 2, PrimeFaces,
    RESTful Web Services, Spring, Hibernate, Cloud Computing with Hadoop, Ajax with jQuery, Android development, and so forth. For more details, please see
    <a href="http://courses.coreservlets.com/">the training courses overview</a> or
    contact Marty at <a href="mailto:hall@coreservlets.com">hall@coreservlets.com</a>.</li>
  <li><b>Consulting, mentoring and small-scale onsite software development.</b> 
    <a href="http://courses.coreservlets.com/about-instructor.html">Marty Hall</a>
    is available for relatively short-term consulting, mentoring and development, anywhere in 
    USA and Canada. For details, contact Marty at 
    <a href="mailto:hall@coreservlets.com">hall@coreservlets.com</a>.</li>
  <li><b>Teaming and large-scale software development.</b> Coreservlets.com is a partner with 
    <a href="http://www.elegantts.com/">ETS (Elegant Technical Solutions, Inc)</a>.
    ETS is a certified small business in Maryland with an experienced team of 
    J2EE architects and developers, most of whom have high-level government security clearances. 
    They specialize in complex projects for DOD clients, the intelligence community,
    and commercial organizations. We are available for joint bids, 
    short and long-term consulting, offsite software development, mentoring, and more. Experienced architects 
    and developers with JSF 2, Ajax, Spring, Hibernate, JPA, RESTful Web Services, Hadoop, Ajax (with jQuery), 
    servlets, JSP, GWT, and other Java-related technologies.
    Available primarily in the Baltimore/Washington area. For details,
    contact Marty at <a href="mailto:hall@coreservlets.com">hall@coreservlets.com</a>.</li>
  <li><b>Low-cost outsourced software development.</b> We partner with a reputable and
    experienced outsourcing organization in the Philippines. The difference from other
    outsourcing options: <a href="http://courses.coreservlets.com/about-instructor.html">Marty</a> <i>personally</i>
    meets with your organization and provides technical direction and oversight
    to the Manila-based team. Available at any location worldwide. For details, contact Marty at 
    <a href="mailto:hall@coreservlets.com">hall@coreservlets.com</a>.</li>
  <li><b>Excess food disposal.</b> Have extra pepperoni pizza, sashimi, tacos al pastor, or
