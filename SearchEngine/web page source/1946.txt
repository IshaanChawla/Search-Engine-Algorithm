	<h1 class="book_title">More Servlets and JavaServer Pages</h1>

<h2 class="content_subheading">Note: the complete text of
  the <u>second</u> edition of <i>Core Servlets and JavaServer Pages</i>
  is also online for free access. Please see
  <a href="http://pdf.coreservlets.com/">http://pdf.coreservlets.com/</a>.
</h2>
<br/>
<table bgcolor="yellow" align="center"><tr><th>
  <p class="content_heading">
  <font color="red" size="-1">
    There is now a free online servlet and JSP tutorial based on the book. The tutorial has complete source code that has been <i>updated</i> to the latest
    servlet and JSP versions, uses Eclipse, and has exercises and exercise solutions.
