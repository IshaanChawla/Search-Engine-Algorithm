settings.
&#x2013; Note that setPaint and getPaint supersede the setColor

    Shear Transformations
&#x2022; Meaning of Shear
&#x2013; X shear
If you specify a non-zero x shear, then x values will be
more and more shifted to the right the farther they go in
the y direction. For example, an x shear of 0.1 means that
the x value will be shifted 10% of the distance the point is
away from the x axis.
&#x2013; Y shear
Points are shifted down in proportion to the distance they
are to the right (x direction) from the y axis.
&#x2013; Note
&#x2022; Called &#x201C;skew&#x201D; instead of &#x201C;shear&#x201D; in some graphics libraries
(e.g., with Android drawing)
