<P>This type is loop is almost identical to the <a href="php5p4.html">while loop</a>, 
  except that the condition comes at the end:</P>
<P class="indent_big">do</P>
<p class="indent_bigger">statement</p>
<p class="indent_big">while (condition)</p>
<p>The difference is that your statement gets executed at least once. In a normal 
  while loop, the condition could be met before your statement gets executed. 
</p>
<p>Don&#146;t worry too much about do &#133; while loops. Concentrate on <a href="php5p1.html">For 
  loops</a> and <a href="php5p4.html">While loops</a>. But there is another type 
  of loop that comes in handy - the <a href="php6p5.html">For Each loop</a>. First, 
  a quick word about the <b>break</b> statement.</p>
