	<div id="freesupport">
		<h3>Can you please help me for free with a question which is not related to your tutorials?</h3>
		
		As a rule of thumb we don't respond to questions if they are not related to vogella tutorials. The reason is that 
		we don't have the time.
		
		<p>
			We suggest that you use the relevant Java forums, mailing lists or newsgroups.
			For example, if you are looking for help in Eclipse, the <a href="http://www.eclipse.org/forums/"> Eclipse forums</a> have
			many resources for people interested in using and extending Eclipse.
		</p>
		
		The following are examples for question we tend not to answer:

		<ul>
			<li>I'm trying to extend your example, but it doesn't work. Can you please help me?</li>
			<li>I'm trying to do something similar to your example, but it doesn't work. Can you please help me?</li>
			<li>I'm trying something (which is not part of your tutorials), but it doesn't work. Can you please help me?</li>
			<li> I would like to do something (which is not part of your tutorials). Can you please tell me how?</li>
		</ul>
