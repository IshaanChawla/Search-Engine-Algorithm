						<div style="float: right; margin-left: 30px; width: 462px;"><img src="/courses/this-course-at-mit/this-course-list-2col.png" alt="A sample of a This Course at MIT page." style="width: 462px; height: 263px;" /><br /> <p class="caption">This Course at MIT pages provide context for how the course materials published on OCW were used at MIT. The image above shows the This Course at MIT page for <a href="/courses/nuclear-engineering/22-033-nuclear-systems-design-project-fall-2011/this-course-at-mit">22.033 <em>Nuclear Systems Design Project</em></a>.</p></div> <p>This Course at MIT pages provide context for how the course materials published on OCW were originally used at MIT. We hope that such information will help you better understand and use the course materials.</p> <p>These add-on pages are structured like fact sheets, providing key information in a concise manner that is standardized across courses. The pages may include sections such as</p> <div style="font-size: 1.1em"><ul style="margin-left: 5px;">     <li>Course Outcomes</li>     <li>Curriculum Information</li>     <li>The Classroom</li>     <li>Student Information</li>     <li>How Student Time Was Spent</li>     <li>Instructor Insights</li>     <li>Course Team Roles</li> </ul></div> <p>Some of these pages feature an Instructor Insights section, in which MIT instructors share their experiences, ideas, and approaches as educators. We hope the Instructor Insights section will particularly interest educators who are reflecting on their own practice.</p> <p>This Course at MIT is part of our <a href="/educator">OCW Educator initiative</a>, which aims to enhance the value of OCW materials for educators.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><em>The following are direct links to This Course at MIT pages.</em></p>
