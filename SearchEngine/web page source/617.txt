<h2>Are you a Book Author, Teacher or Content Writer?</h2>
<p>If you have great experience in any of the below mentioned domain and you believe that you are exceptionally good in presenting self learning content in beautiful and attractive layout then we provide golden opportunity to earn a great salary sitting at home and producing high quality tutorials for us.</p>
<ul class="list">
<li><p>Information Technology</p></li>
<li><p>Software Quality management</p></li>
<li><p>Java related technologies</p></li>
<Li><p>Mainframe related technologies</p></li>
<li><p>Web development related technologies</p></li>
<li><p>Project Management</p></li>
<li><p>Accounting and Finance</p></li>
<li><p>Telecommunication</p></li>
<li><p>Big Data</p></li>
<li><p>Microsoft Technologies</p></li>
<li><p>Wordpress, Joomla CMS</p></li>
<li><p>Open Sources</p></li>
<li><p>Embedded Systems</p></li>
<li><p>Soft Skills</p></li>
<li><p>Academic Curriculums</p></li>
<li><p>Any other subject where you can write great content....</p></li>
</ul>
<h2>How it works?</h2>
<blockquote>First of all, you are requested to produce a sample write-up of 2-3 pages to show your writing capabilities and command over the chosen subject.</blockquote>
<p>If you qualify based on the provided sample write-up, following are the steps to be followed:</p>
<ul class="list">
<li><p>We will work with you to prepare a table of content (ToC) for the subject on which you are going to write a tutorial.</p></li>
<li><p>Based on finalized ToC of the tutorial, you as an author will give us a proposal indicating total cost of the tutorial best on your estimated effort and tentative delivery timelines for the complete work.</p></li>
<li><p>If a discussion is required then it will be discussed, and total cost of the tutorial along with tentative  delivery timelines will be finalized.</p></li>
<li><p>Author will start working on the tutorial and keep sharing the finalized content for a technical and presentation review.</p></li>
<li><p>Tutorials Point will provide necessary comments for the improvement which will be incorporated by the author before final delivery of the tutorial.</p></li>
<li><p>Once tutorial is completed and delivered with the desired quality, finalized cost of the tutorial will be paid to the author either through bank to bank transaction or through Paypal.</p></li>
<li><p>There is also an arrangement to divide whole payment in different installments based on content delivery milestones.</p></li>
</ul>
<h2>Content Copyright</h2>
<p>Once a tutorial is accepted and published at our website tutorialspoint.com, Tutorials Point (India) Private Limited holds complete copyright of the content and same content can not be re-used by the author for any further online or offline publishing.</p>
<p>We respect our tutorial writers and assume they are great subject matter experts. We expect them to write thier tutorials based on their experience and expertise only. We strictly follow copyright laws and hence, we do not accept any content which has been copied from any other websites, books or other copyrighted resources.</p>
<h2>How much you can earn?</h2>
<p>It very much depends on various factors like length of the tutorial, complexity of the tutorial, popularity of the subject etc. but in general we pay in the range of $500 to $1500 per tutorial.</p>
<p>You have to consider an important factor which is that if you are good in writing and deliver as per standard set by Tutorials Point, then you will be assigned on next tutorial just immediately after completion of a tutorial. So there is no waiting time and no limit on your earnings.</p>
<h2>How to Apply?</h2>
<p>Kindly send your profile along with suitable write-up at <b>contact@tutorialspoint.com</b> mentioning the subject where you want to write. Further more, a tutorial construction is based on one time fixed cost and there is no roality or any other incentives will be paid to the author for the delivered content.</p>
