<h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<hr class="tall">
<p>We come across various questions from our keen and enthusiastic readers. Here, we are answering few most important questions.</p>
<section class="toggle">
<label>Who started tutorialspoint.com? When? Who owns it?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>The  tutorialspoint.com is a dedicated website to provide quality online education in the domains of Computer Engineering academics, Information Technology, Computer Languages and Management. This website is started by an AMU alumni, Mr. Mohtashim, by launching a single tutorial on HTML in year 2006.
At present, this website is developed and maintained by Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd., which established on 12th June, 2014. (Twelfth day of June Two Thousand Fourteen), under the Companies Act, 2013. The CIN of the company is <b>“U80904AP2014PTC094598”</b></p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>Where is Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd. located?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>We are located in Hyderabad, the beautiful city of Nizam. Our postal address is –</p>

<ul class="contact">
<li><p><i class="icon icon-map-marker"></i> <strong>Postal Address:</strong> Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd., 388-A , Road no 22, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh-500033, <b>INDIA</b></p></li>
<li><p><i class="icon icon-envelope"></i> <strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:contact@tutorialspoint.com">contact@tutorialspoint.com</a></p></li>
<li><p><i class="icon icon-dribbble"></i> <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com">www.tutorialspoint.com</a></p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>Can we rely on your tutorials and trainings in terms of their quality?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>To answer this, we would like to say that it’s our business to provide tutorials and trainings either absolutely free or at very nominal cost. We are striving hard to keep our tutorials and training quality as high as possible. Our tutorials and training material are being used by lot of freelancer trainers and training companies as well to teach their students.</p>
<p>We are confident on our teaching quality and that is the reason giving guarantee that if you do not like our training then you can demand for complete refund even after 2 attending 2 classes.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>What is main source of income for the website?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>The tutorialspoint.com is primarily funded by the founder of Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd. We also earn enough revenue from the advertisements published on the website, trainings provided online and by selling ebooks. We are financially strong company but we look forward to have trusted investors join hands with us and become part of our mission.</p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="toggle">
<label>Do you provide any live support to help your users?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>Yes we do provide live support. You can contact us using given email ID, we also provide a toll free number <b>1-800-200-9249</b> (reachable from India) to help you in finding required tutorials and training schedule. There is also a <b>Live Chat Support</b> available where any user can talk directly to our customer service representative, who should be able to answer your queries on the spot. Though it’s not possible for us to answer on the technical queries but still we try to help our readers and trainees as much as possible.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>Where is tutorialspoint.com hosted?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>Initially, tutorialspoint.com was hosted with Godaddy.com. In late 2010, the website was transported with the most reliable hosting provider named Peer1, where we have set up more powerful server to handle a large number of users.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>What is server configuration for tutorialspoint.com?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>Currently tutorialspoint.com is running on a dedicated server.</p>
<p>Server Configuration -</p>
<p><b>Dual Xeon E5-2630 2.3 GHz Six -Core 12 Threads (w H/T)</b> server with <b>Kingston SSD</b> behind a <b>Juniper SSG 5</b> firewall.</p>
<p>Running on <b>Apache/2.2.15</b> on <b>CentOS release 6.5</b> and provided forum is supported by <b>phpBB-3</b></p>
<p><b>CMS - WordPress,</b> a powerful, reliable and simple CMS for managing short articles.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>What is current traffic on tutorialspoint.com?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>At present,  tutorialspoint.com is  read by 13 million students and professionals around the world who  access almost 35 million pages every month. 
There is rise in the number of our users by double every year.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>Who writes the contents for tutorialspoint.com?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>The content on the website are created by highly skilled professionals. A number of freelancers helped us  in the growth of the contents. They contributed their best tutorials into our Shared Tutorials Dictionary.   We have established permanent setup in Hyderabad, INDIA, where we have  a dedicated team of subject matters experts, technical writers,  web developers and graphics designers who are taking website to its next height of excellence.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>Can we reuse/publish the content available on tutorialspoint.com?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>Sorry, we do not allow to use our content for republishing online or to be printed for commercial purpose. But yes you can use our content with in your Schools, Colleges, Universities, Institutes and Companies as long as its only for internal purpose and you are keeping appropriate references to tutorialspoint.com website.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>What is mission of tutorialspoint.com? </label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>Our mission is to deliver <b>Simply Easy Learning</b> with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of technical and non-technical subjects without any preconditions and impediments.</p>
</div>
</section>

<section class="toggle">
<label>How I can work for the website?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>If you wish to contribute, you are welcome. You can work with tutorialspoint.com as a freelancer, as well as a permanent employee at our office in Hyderabad-INDIA. 
To know about openings, visit the link – <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com/about/about_careers.htm">Careers at Tutorials Point</a></p>
</div>
</section>


<section class="toggle">
<label>How I can help tutorialspoint.com?</label>
<div class="toggle-content">
<p>It will be a great help to us if you can point out any typing errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect grammar used in any of the tutorials. Your contribution is value adding and it will certainly help bring further improvement in our service. </p>
<p>You can send your findings/suggestions at - <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com/about/contact_us.htm">Contact us</a></p>

</div>
