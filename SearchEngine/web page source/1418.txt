							<h2 class="subhead">Help build OCW's future one course at a time</h2> <p>OCW is built one course at a time when faculty donate their course  materials for open publication. Often the materials they share represent  a lifetime of scholarship and teaching, and their gift is an act of  intellectual philanthropy that touches the lives of millions worldwide.</p> <p>Now, through our Course Champions program, you can demonstrate your  commitment to knowledge as a public good by providing annual support for  one of OCW's courses. With your gift, you join the MIT faculty in  building OCW course by course.</p> <p><strong>Annual Support</strong></p> <p>Your Course Champion Gift of $5,000 provides one year of support for  the course of your choice on OCW site.</p> <p>Course Champions providing annual support receive:</p> <ul>     <li>Acknowledgment by name on the course of your choice for one year</li>     <li>A personalized message from you linked from your course</li>     <li>Acknowledgment, including your personal message, in the MIT  OpenCourseWare newsletter</li>     <li>Invitations to on-campus and virtual OCW events</li> </ul> <p>Your gift supports:</p> <ul>     <li>Ongoing delivery of your course</li>     <li>Delivery of OCW to developing regions</li>     <li>Teaching and technology innovation in the department of your course</li> </ul> <p>Consider a gift of $15,000 or $25,000 to support several courses from  a favorite professor or to support a favorite course for multiple  years. We also welcome Course Champion support from groups of  supporters, clubs, foundations and other charitable organizations.</p> <p>Contact <a href="mailto:ocw-outreach@mit.edu">ocw-outreach@mit.edu</a>  for more information.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p>

<h2 class="name">MIT Alumni</h2>

<p>In every student's time at MIT, there is a professor &ndash; or sometimes a course &ndash; that defines the experience of being at the Institute.</p>

<p>Years after leaving MIT, most alumni can point to a professor or a course that changed the direction of their career or ignited their interest in a specific field.</p>

<p>Share that special course, the work of that memorable professor, with the world. Through your Course Champion gift, the education that changed your life can change the lives of others.</p>
