<td class="answercell">
    <div class="post-text" itemprop="text">
<p>This is found in Tanna Devei Eliyahu Rabba, <a href="http://www.hebrewbooks.org/pdfpager.aspx?req=49591&amp;st=&amp;pgnum=56&amp;hilite=">beginning of chapter 18</a> and Yalkut Shamoni Eicha <a href="http://www.hebrewbooks.org/pdfpager.aspx?req=9758&amp;st=&amp;pgnum=376&amp;hilite=">1034</a>.</p>

<p>The Tanna Devei Eliyahu version is:</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>??? ???? ???? ??? ???? ??? ?' ???? ???? ?? ?"? ????? ????? ????? ????? ????? ???"? ???? ????? ????? ????? ???</p>
  
  <p>What does "<a href="http://www.chabad.org/library/bible_cdo/aid/16458/jewish/Chapter-2.htm#showrashi=true&amp;v=19">Poor out your heart like water opposite the face of Hashem</a>" mean? From here they said that every Torah scholar that sits and reads and repeats and learns Torah, Hashem sits opposite him and read and repeats with him.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>It then goes on to demonstrate that ??? - which I translated as opposite - means face to face.</p>
